Drewry Route Capacity Database

What trades are covered by RCD?

All direct services for the major East-West, North-South (including Middle East, South Asia) trades. Indirect and intra-regional services are not included at present.

What searches can I make?

By using the QUERY tab users can search for capacity data by the following criteria:

  • Ports
  • Countries
  • Regions (ie West Africa)
  • Vessels
  • Operators
  • Trade Routes
  • Transit times in the Port to Port search function
